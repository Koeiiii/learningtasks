My Top 10 Favorite Filipino Dishes

Chicken Adobo

A savory dish of chicken simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices.

Sinigang

A sour tamarind-based soup with pork, vegetables, and a tangy broth.

Lechon

A whole roasted pig with crispy skin and tender, flavorful meat.

Kare-Kare

A rich peanut stew with oxtail, vegetables, and shrimp paste.

Sisig

A sizzling dish made of chopped pork, liver, and spices.

Bulalo

A beef shank soup with bone marrow, simmered for a rich broth.

Pancit

Stir-fried noodles with vegetables, meat, and soy-based sauce.

Halo-Halo

A refreshing dessert with shaved ice, sweet beans, fruits, and leche flan.

Laing

A spicy dish made from dried taro leaves cooked in coconut milk.

Bicol Express

A spicy pork dish cooked in coconut milk and chili peppers.
